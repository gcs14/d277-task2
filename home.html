Welcome to
Virginia

Welcome to Virginia, the captivating heart of the American South! Nestled between the majestic Blue Ridge Mountains and the sparkling waters of the Chesapeake Bay, Virginia offers a tapestry of experiences for every traveler. Begin your journey in historic Richmond, where cobblestone streets lead to the iconic Capitol building and vibrant arts districts beckon with galleries and theaters. Discover the charm of coastal Virginia in Virginia Beach, with its pristine beaches and lively boardwalk, or immerse yourself in the cultural melting pot of Alexandria, where colonial architecture blends seamlessly with modern shops and eateries. From outdoor enthusiasts to history buffs, foodies to art aficionados, Virginia promises an unforgettable journey filled with beauty, history, and Southern hospitality.

Top cities to visit

Be amazed and take your pick from the diverse options in scenery Virginia provides. Hop from shop to shop among the hustle and bustle of Arlington, learn of the Commonwealth's history at the capital in Richmond, or take it easy soaking up the sun and fun at Virginia Beach. Whatever you're looking for you can find here.

Did you know?

Originally settled by early British Colonists in the 1680s, Tangier Island, located in Virginia's Chesapeake Bay, has a distinct English dialect not found anywhere else in the world.
